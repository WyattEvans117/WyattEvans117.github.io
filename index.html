If you are here I presume you to be a recruiter, client or other interested party who found the link through my resume. Resumes are excellent for quickly analyzing an applicant but the standardization can stifle creativity and make it difficult to gauge important soft skills, such as communication. Please recognize this has been developed with a desktop or laptop experience in mind. While it should work properly even on mobile devices, I do not have the capability to extensively test the site.

I have designed this website by using the waterfall approach to development. This approach relies on initial research. I chose this method over the agile method, which is often preferred by organizations. Agile enjoys a variety of perks such as live and quick feedback. However this method relies on user-feedback and updates to the website. As this website will not gain as much traction as one used by a large business entity I decided to employ the waterfall design philosophy.
